Lessons

  1. Addition

    Addition is combining two or more numbers (or things) together to make a new total values.

    Example: When one muffin is added to another one muffin, we will have two muffins
    Using Numerals it is: 1 + 1 = 2
    And in words it is: "One plus one equals two"

  2. Subtraction

    Subtraction is removing objects from a collection. The result of a subtraction is called a difference.

    Example: When two toast is remove from a group of three toast, there will be only one toast left.
    Using Numerals it is: 3 - 2 = 1
    And in words it is: "Three minus two equals one"
